About DIBBIACA KENNELS

Located in Chicago, IL, DIBBIACA HIGH PERFORMANCE CANE CORSO & BANDOG KENNELS LLC is a veteran-owned kennel with a family legacy of over 40 years in breeding and training elite dogs. Our team also brings experience from law enforcement and service work, ensuring reliable, well-trained companions.

Our Breeds

Cane Corso

Calm, confident, loyal Italian guardians. Excellent family protectors, obedient, emotionally attached to handlers. Ideal for service work and emotional support.

Bandog

High-drive working breed highly capable of protection, hunting, and guarding against all threats. Athletic, muscular, intelligent, and highly trainable, forming strong bonds with family and handlers.

Breed Comparison: Cane Corso vs Bandog

Trait Cane Corso Bandog
Appearance Large, muscular, elegant Larger, robust, built for protection
Weight & Size 90–140+ lbs 120–160+ lbs
Temperament Calm, unwavering loyalty, intelligent, eager to please family High-drive, intense, protective, can be highly independent depending on training
Protection Instinct Controlled, reliable, Fierce Strong instinct, requires experienced handler, Livestock Guardian, moderate to high Prey Drive depending on individual dog
Training Requirements Moderate High
Family Compatibility Excellent with children Bonded but, supervision required around individuals outside of immediate household family
Special Capabilities Home protection, service, obedience Extreme protection, hunting, emotional support

Breed Temperament Comparison

Cane Corso

  • Protective: High, but controlled
  • Loyal: Extremely bonded to family
  • Intelligence: Quick learner, obedient
  • Energy: Moderate
  • Trainability: Responds well to consistent training
  • Socialization: Friendly with family, cautious with strangers

Bandog

  • Protective: Very high, strong instinct
  • Loyal: Deeply bonded to handlers
  • Intelligence: Smart, independent thinker
  • Energy: Very high
  • Trainability: Requires experienced handler
  • Socialization: Needs early exposure and supervision
